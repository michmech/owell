# Licensing

Transcriptions made by volunteers on Opening the Well are available under the [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) (CC BY 4.0) licence. This allows reuse as long as the volunteers who produced the transcriptions are credited. A short statement such as “by Opening the Well contributors” along with a link to Opening the Well will suffice. It is not necessary to credit the transcribers of specific recordings individually.

This licence applies to the written transcriptions only. Visit the main [Tobar an Dualchais/Kist o Riches](https://www.tobarandualchais.co.uk/using-recordings) website for details on re-using the audio recordings.
