# Licensing

Transcriptions made by volunteers on Opening the Well are available under the [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) (CC BY 4.0) licence.

This means that anybody anywhere in the world is allowed to reuse the transcriptions, for any purpose whatsoever, without having to ask for permission.

The only condition is that reusers must give credit to the volunteers who produced the transcriptions. A short statement such as “by Opening the Well contributors” along with a link to Opening the Well will suffice. It is not necessary to credit the transcribers of specific recordings individually.
